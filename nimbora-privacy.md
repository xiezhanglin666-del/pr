# Nimbora — Privacy Policy

**App:** Nimbora · **Bundle ID:** com.xiezg.nimborawetherradar · **Developer:** xiezg · **Effective date:** May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Nimbora iOS application ("Nimbora", "the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App. It applies only to Nimbora and not to any other application published by the developer.

## 1. Information We Collect

Nimbora collects only the categories of data listed below. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, contacts, health data, financial data, or any other sensitive personal information.

| Data category | What it contains | Why we collect it | Linked to your identity? | Used to track you? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Coarse location | Approximate region derived from network signals (not GPS) | To serve relevant ads via Google AdMob | No | Yes (advertising) |
| Device identifier (IDFA) | Apple's Advertising Identifier, only if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt | Third-party advertising via Google AdMob | No | Yes |
| User identifier | Firebase Analytics App Instance ID, generated locally and reset when you uninstall | Aggregate analytics | No | No |
| Product interaction | Anonymous events such as "screen viewed", "city added", "radar opened" | Aggregate analytics | No | No |
| Advertising data | Ad impressions and clicks | Ad delivery and measurement via Google AdMob | No | Yes |
| Crash data | Stack traces and device model at the moment of a crash | To diagnose and fix bugs | No | No |
| Performance data | Launch time, frozen frames, network latency | To improve app performance | No | No |
| Other diagnostics | Non-personal technical signals (e.g. iOS version, locale) | App functionality | No | No |

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **App functionality** — to operate the multi-city list, radar map, scenario modes, weather change notifications, and forecasts.
- **Analytics** — to understand which features are used and where the app crashes, so we can fix and improve it.
- **Advertising** — to serve ads in the App through Google AdMob. Ads keep Nimbora free.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for credit scoring, profiling for legal decisions, or any other purpose outside the categories listed above.

## 3. Location

If a future version of the App requests your precise location, it will do so only via the standard iOS permission prompt, only to look up the weather for your current city, and only while you have granted permission. Precise GPS coordinates are never sent to AdMob. The current v1.0 release uses coarse, network-derived location only, exclusively through the AdMob SDK.

## 4. Tracking and the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) Prompt

On first launch you will see Apple's standard ATT permission prompt asking whether Nimbora may track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. If you allow tracking, AdMob may use the IDFA to deliver more relevant ads. If you decline, AdMob will only serve non-personalized ads. Your choice does not change which features of the App are available to you.

## 5. Third-Party SDKs

Nimbora uses the following third-party SDKs. Each SDK has its own privacy practices:

- **Google AdMob** — advertising. <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>
- **Firebase Analytics (Google)** — analytics and crash reporting. <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>

## 6. Data Retention

Analytics and advertising data are retained according to the retention policies of Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics (typically 14 to 26 months for event-level data, longer for aggregated data). Crash and performance data are retained by Firebase for up to 90 days at the event level. Nimbora itself does not store any of your personal data on its own servers — we do not operate a backend that stores user accounts.

## 7. Your Rights

### Users in the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. You may also object to processing based on legitimate interests. Because Nimbora does not maintain user accounts, the practical way to exercise these rights is to:

- Deny the ATT prompt to stop IDFA-based advertising tracking.
- Reset your IDFA via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
- Uninstall Nimbora, which removes the on-device Firebase App Instance ID.
- Contact us at the email below to request deletion of any analytics records associated with your App Instance ID, if you can provide it.

### Users in California (CCPA / CPRA)

You have the right to know what categories of personal information are collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to correct inaccurate personal information, and the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Nimbora does not sell personal information for money. Where advertising via AdMob may constitute "sharing" under CPRA, declining the ATT prompt operates as an opt-out.

## 8. Children

Nimbora is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 9. International Transfers

Data collected by Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics may be processed on servers in the United States and other countries where Google operates. Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses and other lawful transfer mechanisms for EEA/UK data.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top will be updated. Material changes will be highlighted in the App's "What's New" notes for the version that introduces them.

# Nimbora — 隐私政策（简体中文）

**应用名称：**Nimbora · **Bundle ID：**com.xiezg.nimborawetherradar · **开发者：**xiezg · **生效日期：**2026 年 5 月 12 日

本隐私政策说明 Nimbora iOS 应用（以下简称"Nimbora""本应用""我们"）在你使用本应用时如何收集、使用和共享信息。本政策仅适用于 Nimbora，不适用于本开发者发布的其他任何应用。

## 1. 我们收集的信息

Nimbora 仅收集下表列出的数据类别。我们不收集姓名、邮箱、电话、照片、通讯录、健康数据、财务数据或其他任何敏感个人信息。

| 数据类别 | 具体内容 | 收集目的 | 是否关联身份 | 是否用于追踪 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 粗略位置 | 由网络信号推断的大致区域（非 GPS） | 通过 Google AdMob 投放相关广告 | 否 | 是（广告） |
| 设备标识符（IDFA） | 仅在你通过 ATT 弹窗授权后采集 Apple 的广告标识符 | 通过 Google AdMob 进行第三方广告 | 否 | 是 |
| 用户标识符 | Firebase Analytics 应用实例 ID，本地生成，卸载后重置 | 聚合分析 | 否 | 否 |

## 2. 如何使用

- **App 功能** — 运行多城市列表、雷达地图、场景模式、天气变化提醒和预报。
- **分析** — 了解功能使用情况与崩溃位置，以便修复与改进。
- **广告** — 通过 Google AdMob 在 App 内投放广告以维持免费。

我们不出售你的个人信息，也不用于信用评分、法律相关画像或上述以外的任何用途。

## 3. 位置

如未来版本需要精确位置，将仅通过 iOS 标准权限弹窗征得同意，用于查询当前城市的天气，且只在你授权期间使用。精确 GPS 坐标不会被发送至 AdMob。当前 v1.0 仅通过 AdMob SDK 使用基于网络的粗略位置。

## 4. 追踪与 ATT 弹窗

首次启动时会出现 Apple 标准的 ATT 权限弹窗，询问 Nimbora 是否可以跨其他公司的 App 与网站追踪你。允许则 AdMob 可使用 IDFA 投放更相关的广告；拒绝则 AdMob 只投放非个性化广告。无论你做何选择，所有功能均可正常使用。

## 5. 第三方 SDK

- **Google AdMob** — 广告。<https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>
- **Firebase Analytics（Google）** — 分析与崩溃报告。<https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>

## 6. 数据保留

分析与广告数据按照 Google AdMob 与 Firebase Analytics 的保留策略保留（事件级通常 14–26 个月，聚合数据更长）。崩溃与性能数据由 Firebase 在事件级别最长保留 90 天。Nimbora 本身不在自有服务器存储任何个人数据 — 我们没有运营存储用户账号的后端。

## 7. 你的权利

### 欧洲经济区、英国、瑞士用户（GDPR）

你享有访问、更正、删除、限制处理你的个人数据的权利，以及数据可携权与基于合法利益的反对权。由于 Nimbora 不维护用户账号，可通过以下方式行使权利：

- 拒绝 ATT 弹窗，停止基于 IDFA 的广告追踪。
- 在 iOS "设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 追踪" 中重置 IDFA。
- 卸载 Nimbora 即可清除设备上的 Firebase 应用实例 ID。
- 通过下方邮箱联系我们，提供你的应用实例 ID 以请求删除相关分析记录。

### 加州用户（CCPA / CPRA）

你享有知情权、删除权、更正权，以及对"出售"或为跨上下文行为广告而"共享"个人信息的退出权。Nimbora 不会以金钱出售个人信息。如通过 AdMob 的广告行为构成 CPRA 意义下的"共享"，拒绝 ATT 弹窗即视为退出。

## 8. 儿童

Nimbora 并非面向 13 周岁以下（或你所在司法辖区的最低同等年龄）的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童个人信息。若你认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们以便删除。

## 9. 跨境传输

Google AdMob 与 Firebase Analytics 收集的数据可能在美国及 Google 运营的其他国家或地区处理。对于欧洲经济区/英国数据，Google 依据标准合同条款及其他合法跨境传输机制处理。

## 10. 政策变更

我们可能不时更新本政策。顶部的"生效日期"将相应更新。重大变更会在引入该变更的版本的"此版本新增内容"中提示。

# Weather Radar – Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 15, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Weather Radar** iOS app ("the App", "we") handles your information. The App is designed to collect as little data as possible.

## 1. Information We Access

### Location (only with your permission)
- The App requests **"While Using"** location access via Apple's standard system prompt.
- Location is used **only** to fetch local weather, nearby radar imagery, and short-term rain alerts for your current position.
- Your coordinates are sent to the weather data provider for the duration of a request and are **not stored** by the App or transmitted to any server we operate.
- You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

### Device identifier for advertising (only with your permission)
- The App displays ads served by **Google AdMob**.
- If you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission, AdMob may access your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to show more relevant ads.
- If you decline ATT, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized using IDFA.
- ATT permission can be changed in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

### Diagnostic information
- We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., app opens, screen views) that help us improve the App. This data is not linked to your identity.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not require account registration or login.
- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.
- We do not sell or rent any of your data.

## 3. Local Storage

Your preferences (temperature unit, wind unit, time format, etc.) are stored **only on your device** using `UserDefaults`. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies:

| Service | Purpose | Policy |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Open-Meteo | Weather data | https://open-meteo.com/en/terms |
| Apple CoreLocation | Geocoding (lat/lon ↔ city name) | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |
| Google AdMob | Advertising | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Firebase Analytics | Anonymous usage analytics | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

## 7. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us at: **xiezhanglin666@gmail.com**

# 天气雷达 – 隐私政策

**更新日期:2026 年 5 月 15 日**

本隐私政策说明 **天气雷达(Weather Radar)** iOS App(下称"本 App"、"我们")如何处理您的信息。我们在设计上尽可能减少数据收集。

## 1. 我们访问的信息

### 位置信息(仅在您授权后)
- 本 App 通过 Apple 标准系统弹窗请求 **"使用 App 期间"** 的位置权限。
- 位置信息**仅**用于获取当前位置的天气、附近雷达图像和短临降雨提醒。
- 您的坐标仅在请求期间发送给天气数据服务,本 App **不存储**,也不会传输到我们运营的任何服务器。
- 您可以随时在 iOS 设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 定位服务 中撤销该权限。

### 广告标识符(仅在您授权后)
- 本 App 通过 **Google AdMob** 展示广告。
- 如果您同意"App 跟踪透明度(ATT)"权限,AdMob 可能会访问您的广告标识符(IDFA),用于展示更相关的广告。
- 如果您拒绝 ATT,仍会展示广告,但不会使用 IDFA 进行个性化推送。
- ATT 权限可以在 iOS 设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 跟踪 中随时修改。

### 诊断信息
- 我们使用 Firebase Analytics(Google)收集匿名使用统计(如 App 启动次数、页面访问)以帮助改进产品。此类数据不会与您的身份关联。

## 2. 我们不收集的信息

- 我们不要求注册账号或登录。
- 我们不收集您的姓名、邮箱、电话、通讯录、照片、麦克风或相机数据。
- 我们不出售或租赁您的任何数据。

## 3. 本地存储

您的偏好设置(温度单位、风速单位、时间制式等)**仅存储在您的设备本地**(通过 `UserDefaults`),不会上传到我们的任何服务器。

## 4. 第三方服务

本 App 使用以下第三方服务,其数据处理遵循各自的隐私政策:

| 服务 | 用途 | 政策链接 |

| Open-Meteo | 天气数据 | https://open-meteo.com/en/terms |
| Apple CoreLocation | 地理编码(经纬度 ↔ 城市名) | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |
| Google AdMob | 广告 | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Firebase Analytics | 匿名使用统计 | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

## 5. 儿童隐私

本 App 不面向 13 岁以下儿童,也不会主动收集儿童的个人信息。

## 6. 政策变更

我们可能不定期更新本政策。重大变更将通过更新上方"更新日期"体现。
